Welcome to IKE

The Movie/Screening Selection Tool for Groups of Friends, Co-Workers, or Families

Make the most of your group's visit to Circle Cinema

Which get-together would you prefer?

IKE helps your group decide upon a movie (and screening day/time) they would most want to watch using ranked-choice voting.
IKE makes it easy for your group to make the best decision (and helps everyone buy their tickets).

If you're the host, all you need to do is create an event and then invite by e-mail your friends, family, or co-workers to rank the movies that they would most like to see.
